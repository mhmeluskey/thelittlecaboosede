How We Involve Parents:


We know that nothing is more important that feeling connected to your children when you can't be there. The Little Caboose includes Tadpoles, a digital app, FREE for families to connect digitally throughout the day.

We are digitally connecting families throughout the day!


This information is safe, secure, and portable so our teachers can update it on the go, and you can receive those updates wherever you are. You can access the info from a smartphone or device, as well as by email.

It's not just parents that want to feel included! Parents, Grandparents, Aunts, and Uncles...whomever you want to feel included can have access to the daily reports and fun photos and videos we may share.

This is one more way that The Little Caboose keeps our families close.
